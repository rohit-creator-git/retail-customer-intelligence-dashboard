# 📊 Power BI Dashboard

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Sales
- Profit Margin

## Visualizations

- Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
- Profit by Region (Column Chart)
- Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)
- Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- Top 10 Products (Bar Chart)
- Top 10 States (Map / Bar Chart)

## Dashboard Filters

- Category
- Region
- Segment
- Ship Mode

## Business Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales.
- Consumer segment contributed the maximum revenue.
- Discounts reduced profit in some categories.
- Western region recorded strong sales performance.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- SQL (MySQL)
- Python
- Microsoft Excel
